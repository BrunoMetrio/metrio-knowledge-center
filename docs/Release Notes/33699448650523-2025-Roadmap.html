2025 Roadmap

Updated February 26, 2025 14:25

Our focus this year

As the regulatory landscape continues to evolve, particularly with the rise of CSRD, our product roadmap this year will be heavily weighted towards providing comprehensive support for CSRD reporting and GHG accounting. In addition, we'll be focusing on enhancing our existing products to better support the diverse and complex reporting workflows our users require.

 

H1 2025

  • Expand our library of Carbon Accounting content with Scope 3
  • Enable task management for narrative content
  • New role and permission management
  • Improve report drafting tools with version control and track changes
  • Enable additional engagement with chat system

H2 2025

  • Enhance CSRD reporting capabilities and user experience
  • AI content generation for CSRD
  • Additional regulatory standards
  • Enhance GHG calculation and reporting capabilities and user experience
  • Enhance connection between DMA and CSRD reporting
  • Strengthen audit trail

 

Important Considerations and Next Steps:

Timeline Flexibility: Please note that these are our current plans, and timelines are subject to change based on ongoing development and user feedback. We will keep you informed of any significant adjustments.

Feedback Welcome: We encourage you to share your feedback on these planned features and priorities.

We are excited about these upcoming releases and their potential to enhance your reporting capabilities. Thank you for your continued support.
